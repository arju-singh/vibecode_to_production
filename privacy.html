Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Arju Singh ("we", "us", "our"), operator of The Vibe Coding Roadmap (the "Site"), collects, uses, and protects information when you visit the Site or make a one-time purchase. By using the Site you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Payment information. When you pay the one-time fee, the transaction is processed by Razorpay. We do not collect or store your card number, UPI ID, CVV, or bank credentials — these are handled directly by Razorpay under their own privacy policy. We receive only a payment ID, order ID, and payment status to confirm your purchase.
  • Local data. Your checklist and roadmap progress is stored in your browser's localStorage on your own device. It never leaves your browser and is not sent to us.
  • Technical data. Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) may be recorded for security, rate-limiting, and abuse prevention.

2. How we use information

  • To process and confirm your one-time payment and grant access to the content.
  • To protect the Site against fraud, abuse, and automated attacks (rate limiting).
  • To comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

3. Cookies & local storage

The Site uses minimal browser storage. We do not set advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Razorpay may set cookies necessary to complete a payment. Your progress data is kept in localStorage and can be cleared at any time from your browser settings.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share payment-related data only with Razorpay (our payment processor) and, where legally required, with tax or regulatory authorities.

5. Data retention

Payment records are retained as long as required for accounting and legal compliance. Server logs are retained for a limited period for security purposes.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data we hold about you, subject to legal retention requirements. To make a request, contact us at the address below.

7. Third-party services

The Site loads fonts from Google Fonts and the payment widget from Razorpay. These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
