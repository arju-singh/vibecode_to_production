Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This policy applies to the one-time purchase of The Vibe Coding Roadmap, a digital product operated by Arju Singh.

1. Digital product

The Roadmap is a digital good delivered instantly upon successful payment. Because access to the full Content is granted immediately, the purchase is generally non-refundable once access has been provided.

2. Cancellation

This is a single one-time payment, not a subscription. There is nothing recurring to cancel, and no future charges will be made.

3. When we will issue a refund

We will review and, where appropriate, refund in the following cases:

  • You were charged more than once for the same purchase (duplicate transaction).
  • Payment was deducted but access was not granted due to a technical fault on our side.
  • An unauthorised or fraudulent transaction is confirmed.

4. How to request a refund

Email connect@arjusingh.com within 7 days of the transaction with your Razorpay payment ID and a short description of the issue. We aim to respond within 3–5 business days.

5. How refunds are processed

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method via Razorpay. Depending on your bank or provider, it may take 5–10 business days for the amount to reflect.

6. Contact

For any billing question, email connect@arjusingh.com.
